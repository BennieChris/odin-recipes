These are the steps to make a great cranberry sauce for your families' Thanksgiving Dinner

Cranberry sauce

weirdly enough this only has 3 things needed

  • Fresh cranberries
  • Sugar
  • Orange Juice
  1. Heat orange juice in saucepan
  2. Stir in sugar and cook until it dissolves
  3. Add in cranberries and continue cooking, stirring often, until they start to pop
  4. Remove the sauce from heat and transfer it to a bowl
  5. Let cool, sauce will thicken and be ready
