This great alcoholic beverage is a mimic of Juggernog from a game

Juggernog

  • 2 Teaspoons of condensed milk
  • 1/2cup warm eggnog
  • 1/2cup carbonated water (unflavored such as club soda or perrier)
  • splash of rum
  • red food colouring (optional)
  1. microwave both the condensed milk and egg nog in the microwave till luke warm
  2. pour the condensed milk along with the eggnog and add a splash of rum
  3. top off the glass with club soda (add the food colouring if you wish) stir and enjoy!
