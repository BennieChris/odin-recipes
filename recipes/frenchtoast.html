French Toast Recipe

These are the steps to make the best French toast on the planet!

The following will be a list of stuff we will need;

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tespoons vanilla extract
  • pinch salt
  • 1 lb bread cut into 3/4 inch slices
  • butter for cooking

Steps

  1. To start you will want to whisk the eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a medium sized bowl.
    2. After you will in a large skillet melt about 2 tbalespoons butter over medium heat. While the butter is melting you should dip your bread in the egg mixture. Next you will place your bread slices into the skillet and cook each side till both sides are golden brown. After this you basically top with whatever you would like whether it be raspberries or powdered sugar and it is ready to eat.
